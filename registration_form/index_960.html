4.TERMS & CONDITIONS:(To be filled by only job guarantee students)
1. Attendance should be above 90%. If a lecture is missed then that can be covered by informing the centre co-ordinator and attending in other batches. A maximum of 12 hours of lectures can be attended in other batches in the entire course.
2. Assignment completion should be 100% and on-time. Faculties will be available to solve any doubts on email, what’s app or call during office hours. It is the duty of the candidate to sign on the attendance sheet and get grades from the faculty on time.
3. Assessment will be conducted from time to time where the candidate needs to score 80 percent aggregate. Re-tests will be available to increase the score.
4. Projects assigned by faculty should be live / deployed to the production Environment as demonstrated by the faculty. Any doubts should be raised to faculty from time to time. Failing to submit the projects within time will delay the completion of the course.
5. Candidates have to attend all interviews suggested / hosted by Itvedant Education Pvt. Ltd. If a candidate is not interested to attend an interview he should take approval from the placement coordinator through email.
6. If the candidate fails to attend 2 or more interviews conducted by itvedant then he/she will be barred from the placement (100% Job Assurance) process.
7. Candidates should prepare all interview Questions and Answers given by Itvedant and have to be a mock interview.
8. Itvedant would not be responsible if the candidate leaves the company or if the employer is firing the candidate for poor performance on misbehaviour.
9.Once a candidate decides to join any company, he will not be eligible for the Next job through Itvedant under the 100% job assurance program.
10. Itvedant will refund job assurance fees collected from students if they fail to give them a job within 6 months after completion of the course excluding the gst tax.
11. 100% Job assurance package does not include a specific amount of salary to Candidate, since the salary negotiation has to be done by candidates in their interview process.
12. Completion of the course is marked when the candidate has completed all the Modules along with the assignment, assessment completes and submits the final project given by the faculty for that module. A master certificate will be issued at the end of the course.
13. A Candidate cannot switch the batch once he is admitted in a batch. However he May be given special exemption under medical and education (exams) grounds subjected to approval from the management. In case of batch change an additional batch change processing fees of ₹3000/- will have to be paid
14. The Candidate needs to pay the fees upfront or as EMI’s with a post-dated Cheque. The candidate will be barred from the course in the case of cheque bounce and needs to pay a penalty of 500/- INR in that case to reinitiate the course.
15. Need to complete Projects assigned by faculty. The projects should be live / deployed to the production environment as instructed by the faculty. Any doubts should be raised to faculty from time to time. Failing to submit the projects within time will delay the completion of the course.
